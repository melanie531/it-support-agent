Acme Corporation — IT Runbook
Hardware Requests & Equipment Management
Last Updated: 2025-05-10
Document Owner: IT Asset Management Team

========================================
1. STANDARD EQUIPMENT
========================================

All new employees receive the following standard equipment:

1.1 Laptop
    - Engineering: MacBook Pro 14" (M3 Pro, 18GB RAM, 512GB SSD) or
      Dell XPS 15 (Intel i7, 32GB RAM, 512GB SSD) — employee choice
    - Non-Engineering: MacBook Air 13" (M3, 16GB RAM, 256GB SSD) or
      Dell Latitude 5540 (Intel i5, 16GB RAM, 256GB SSD)

1.2 Monitor
    - One 27" 4K monitor (Dell U2723QE) provided per employee
    - Additional monitors can be requested with manager approval

1.3 Peripherals
    - Wireless keyboard and mouse
    - USB-C hub/dock (for multi-monitor setups)
    - Headset (Jabra Evolve2 55) for video calls
    - Laptop stand

========================================
2. HOW TO REQUEST NEW HARDWARE
========================================

Step 1: Go to https://jira.acmecorp.com/servicedesk
Step 2: Select "Hardware Request" from the catalog
Step 3: Choose the type of request:
        - New hire equipment setup
        - Additional hardware (monitor, peripherals)
        - Hardware upgrade (more RAM, larger storage)
        - Replacement for broken/outdated equipment
Step 4: Provide justification and any special requirements
Step 5: Your manager and IT Asset Management must approve
Step 6: Delivery timelines:
        - In-stock items: 3-5 business days
        - Special orders: 2-4 weeks
        - New hire setups: Ready on start date (if requested 2 weeks in advance)

========================================
3. REPLACEMENT POLICY
========================================

3.1 Standard Refresh Cycle
    - Laptops: Replaced every 3 years
    - Monitors: Replaced every 5 years
    - Peripherals: Replaced as needed (on failure)
    - You will receive an email notification 30 days before your device
      is eligible for refresh

3.2 Early Replacement
    Early replacement is approved in these cases:
    - Device is physically damaged beyond repair
    - Device no longer meets performance requirements for your role
    - Battery health is below 50%
    - Security vulnerability that cannot be patched
    Submit an early replacement request through the IT portal with justification.

========================================
4. REMOTE EMPLOYEE HOME OFFICE STIPEND
========================================

Full-time remote employees are eligible for a one-time $500 home office stipend.

Covered items:
- Desk and ergonomic chair
- Monitor riser or standing desk converter
- Webcam, ring light, or other video conferencing accessories
- Surge protector or UPS

NOT covered:
- Internet service bills
- Furniture beyond desk and chair
- Personal computing devices

How to claim:
Step 1: Purchase qualifying items
Step 2: Submit receipts through Concur (Expense > "Home Office Stipend" category)
Step 3: Approval from your manager is automatic for amounts under $500
Step 4: Reimbursement within 2-3 pay cycles

========================================
5. BROKEN EQUIPMENT PROCESS
========================================

5.1 Laptop Issues
    Step 1: Back up your data to OneDrive
    Step 2: Submit a ticket at https://jira.acmecorp.com/servicedesk > "Hardware Issue"
    Step 3: Describe the problem in detail (error messages, when it started, etc.)
    Step 4: IT will assess repair vs. replacement:
            - Under warranty: Sent to manufacturer for repair (5-10 business days)
            - Out of warranty: Replacement issued
    Step 5: Request a loaner device while yours is being repaired (see Section 6)

5.2 Monitor / Peripheral Issues
    - Submit a ticket through the IT portal
    - Broken peripherals are typically replaced within 1-2 business days
    - Broken monitors: 3-5 business days for replacement

5.3 Accidental Damage
    - Report immediately through IT portal
    - Accidental damage is covered for the first incident per year
    - Repeated damage may require department cost center approval

========================================
6. LOANER DEVICES
========================================

Loaner laptops are available while your primary device is being repaired:

- Request through IT Help Desk: helpdesk@acmecorp.com or ext. 5000
- Available on a first-come, first-served basis
- Standard configuration: MacBook Air or Dell Latitude with basic software
- Must be returned within 2 business days of receiving your repaired/replacement device
- All data on loaner devices is wiped upon return

========================================
7. RETURNING EQUIPMENT
========================================

When leaving the company or receiving a replacement device:
- Return all IT equipment to the IT Help Desk (Building A, Room 102)
- Remote employees: Use the prepaid shipping label provided by IT
- Ensure you've backed up personal files (with manager approval if applicable)
- Equipment must be returned by your last day of employment
