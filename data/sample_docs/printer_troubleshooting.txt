========================================
2. ADDING A NETWORK PRINTER
========================================

2.1 Windows
    Step 1: Open Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Printers & scanners
    Step 2: Click "Add device"
    Step 3: Click "Add manually" if the printer isn't found automatically
    Step 4: Select "Add a printer using a TCP/IP address"
    Step 5: Enter the printer's hostname (e.g., PRINTER-A2-CLR.acmecorp.internal)
    Step 6: Windows will auto-detect the driver; if not, select "HP Universal Print Driver"
    Step 7: Name the printer and click "Finish"

2.2 macOS
    Step 1: Open System Settings > Printers & Scanners
    Step 2: Click the "+" button to add a printer
    Step 3: Select the "IP" tab
    Step 4: Enter the printer address: PRINTER-A2-CLR.acmecorp.internal
    Step 5: Protocol: HP Jetdirect — Socket
    Step 6: Name: Give it a descriptive name (e.g., "Floor 2 Color")
    Step 7: Driver: Select "HP Universal Print Driver" or "Generic PostScript"
    Step 8: Click "Add"

Note: You must be connected to the corporate network (in-office or via VPN) to
add and use network printers.

3. COMMON PRINT ISSUES
========================================

3.1 Printer Shows "Offline"
    Step 1: Check if the printer's display panel shows any errors
    Step 2: Verify you're on the corporate network (Wi-Fi: AcmeCorp or VPN)
    Step 3: Try removing and re-adding the printer
    Step 4: Restart the Print Spooler service:
            Windows: Open Services > "Print Spooler" > Restart
            macOS: System Settings > Printers & Scanners > right-click printer > Reset printing system

3.2 Paper Jam
    Step 1: Open the front panel and any trays indicated on the printer display
    Step 2: Gently pull out jammed paper in the direction of paper flow
    Step 3: Check for small torn pieces of paper
    Step 4: Close all panels and trays
    Step 5: The printer should automatically resume
    Step 6: If the jam persists, contact Facilities at facilities@acmecorp.com

3.3 Print Driver Errors
    Step 1: Go to https://support.hp.com/drivers
    Step 2: Search for "HP Universal Print Driver" (PCL6)
    Step 3: Download and install the latest version
    Step 4: Remove and re-add the printer with the new driver
    Alternatively, use the HP Smart app for automatic driver management.

3.4 Print Jobs Stuck in Queue
    Step 1: Open the print queue:
            Windows: Settings > Printers > Open print queue
            macOS: System Settings > Printers & Scanners > Open Print Queue
    Step 2: Cancel all stuck jobs
    Step 3: Restart the Print Spooler (see 3.1 Step 4)
    Step 4: Try printing again

========================================
4. SCANNING TO EMAIL
========================================

All "CLR" printers support scan-to-email:

Step 1: Place your document on the scanner glass or in the auto-feeder
Step 2: On the printer touchscreen, select "Scan"
Step 3: Select "Scan to Email"
Step 4: Enter your email address (firstname.lastname@acmecorp.com)
Step 5: Choose scan settings:
        - Format: PDF (default) or JPEG
        - Resolution: 300 DPI (default) or 600 DPI for high quality
        - Color: Auto, Color, or Black & White
Step 6: Press "Scan"
Step 7: The scanned document will arrive in your inbox within 1-2 minutes

========================================
5. TONER & SUPPLY REQUESTS
========================================

Printers are managed by the Facilities team. To request supplies:

- Toner replacement: Submit a request at https://jira.acmecorp.com/servicedesk > "Printer Supplies"
- Paper refills: Each floor has a supply closet (labeled "Print Supplies") — help yourself
- If a supply closet is empty: Email facilities@acmecorp.com
- Toner is typically replaced within 4 hours of a request during business hours

Do NOT attempt to replace toner cartridges yourself unless you've been trained.
Incorrect installation can damage the printer.

========================================
6. DUPLEX & COLOR PRINTING TIPS
========================================

- Duplex (double-sided) printing is enabled by default on all CLR printers to save paper
- To print single-sided: Change the setting in your print dialog before printing
- Color printing: Available on CLR printers only. BW printers will convert color to grayscale.
- For large print jobs (50+ pages): Use the BW printers for faster throughput
- For presentation materials: Use CLR printers with "Best" quality setting
